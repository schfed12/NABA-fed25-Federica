Lesson 8/04/2025 second part units of measurment

  • px, piexel
  • em from "em quad", is a relative unit of measurement
  • rem rem, from "em quad" with a fundamental difference: it is always relative to the size given to the html tag
  • ercentage %, percentage relative to the nearest "position: relative"
  • h and vw, percentage measurements relative to the viewport
